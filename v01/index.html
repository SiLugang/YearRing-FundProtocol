Risk Notice — Whitelist Run Period

  • This protocol is in an early-stage, invite-only whitelist run. It is not open to the public.
  • Participation requires prior approval. Deposits are restricted to allowlisted addresses only.
  • Yield is generated via Aave V3 on Base. Returns are not guaranteed and depend on market conditions.
  • Smart contracts have been tested but are not formally audited. Use only funds you can afford to lose at this stage.
  • Transactions are submitted directly from your wallet. Review each transaction before confirming in MetaMask.
  • The protocol may be paused at any time during this test period. Redemptions remain available to existing shareholders even when deposits are paused.
